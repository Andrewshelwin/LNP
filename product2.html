Perks

Fly ash is a cost-effective alternative to Portland cement, enhancing concrete's durability and workability for improved performance and longevity.

Fly ash prevents cracking, expansion, and reduces permeability while offering resistance to sulphates and alkali-aggregate reactions in concrete.

Fly ash recycles coal combustion waste, reducing CO₂ emissions and promoting environmentally sustainable practices in construction and infrastructure development.
