About Us

Our Company NanoTech Ash Industries was established in 2017 by founder proprietor E. Ariyanayagam Ananth DME. Listed as the first privately owned Flyash manufacturer, the company has made an indelible mark in transforming the industry.

Today, Lakshmi Group is the undisputed market leader in manufacturing high-quality Flyash, concrete, and cement-based value-added products in India. The company operates its certified fly ash and cement concrete testing lab, keeping quality at the forefront of operations.

Our flyash comes with certification for its environmentally friendly manufacturing process, and we have achieved Environment Management Systems Certification and Quality Management Systems Certification.

Warehouse and Factory production Capacity

Our Nano Pozzo warehouse boasts a state-of-the-art facility capable of storing over 20,000 metric tons of stock. This extensive storage capacity ensures a continuous supply of materials to our clients, enabling us to meet their demands without any delays. Our facility is designed to handle high volumes efficiently, ensuring that we can fulfill orders promptly, no matter the size.


In addition to our robust storage capabilities, we also operate an in-house transportation system for flyash supply. This dedicated fleet ensures that deliveries are made on time, maintaining seamless supply chain operations. Our factory’s production capacity exceeds 600 metric tons per day, allowing us to consistently meet the growing demand for our products.


With an emphasis on reliability and efficiency, Nano Pozzo is committed to providing high-quality materials and timely deliveries, ensuring that our clients’ needs are always met.
